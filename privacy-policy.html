Privacy Policy

üîí Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At AQIMap.fun, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our real-time global air quality monitoring platform. The short version: we don't collect, store, or sell your personal information. We believe that monitoring air pollution shouldn't require sacrificing your digital privacy.

This policy applies to all users of aqimap.fun, regardless of geographic location, and is designed to comply with international privacy regulations including GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), and other data protection frameworks worldwide.

üìä Information We Do NOT Collect

Unlike most websites and web applications, AQIMap.fun is designed to function without collecting any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Email Addresses: There's no registration, no newsletter signup, no account system
  • Names or Contact Information: We don't ask for or store your name, phone number, or mailing address
  • Location Data: We don't track your physical location through GPS, IP geolocation, or any other means
  • Payment Information: AQIMap.fun is completely free with no premium features, so we never handle credit cards or payment details
  • Behavioral Tracking: We don't use tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or cross-site cookies to follow your browsing habits
  • User Accounts: There's no sign-up system, so we have no user databases with your personal information

You can use AQIMap.fun completely anonymously. We have no way to identify you personally or link your usage to your real-world identity.

üíæ Local Storage (Browser-Side Data)

While we don't collect data on our servers, AQIMap.fun does store minimal information locally in your web browser to enhance your experience. This data never leaves your device and is under your complete control. Specifically, we store:

Theme Preference

When you toggle between light and dark mode, your preference is saved to your browser's localStorage under the key aqi-theme. This ensures the site remembers your visual preference on future visits. You can clear this at any time through your browser's settings.

AQI Data Cache

To provide instant responses and reduce unnecessary API calls, we temporarily cache air quality data in your browser's memory (sessionStorage) for up to 30 minutes. This cache is automatically cleared when you close the browser tab and contains only anonymized AQI values and country names‚Äîno personal data.

How to Clear Local Data

You can delete all localStorage data at any time by:

  • Chrome/Edge: Settings ‚Üí Privacy and Security ‚Üí Clear Browsing Data ‚Üí Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Cookies and Site Data ‚Üí Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Manage Website Data ‚Üí Remove All

üåê Third-Party Services

World Air Quality Index (WAQI) API

AQIMap.fun retrieves real-time air quality data from the World Air Quality Index project (aqicn.org) through their public API. When you click on a country, your browser makes a direct request to WAQ I's servers to fetch current AQI data. This request includes:

  • The country or city name you're searching for
  • Our API token (not associated with individual users)
  • Standard HTTP headers (browser type, IP address - handled by WAQI, not us)

WAQI maintains its own privacy policy governing how they handle API requests. We recommend reviewing their policy at https://aqicn.org/privacy/.

Google Fonts

AQIMap.fun uses Google Fonts to load the Inter typeface for improved typography. When you visit our site, your browser downloads font files from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. We use the display=swap parameter to prevent render-blocking and improve privacy. Google Fonts' privacy policy is available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

D3.js Library

Our interactive map uses the D3.js visualization library, which we host locally on our servers. No external requests are made to load D3, ensuring this library doesn't introduce privacy concerns.

üç™ Cookies

AQI Map.fun does NOT use cookies. Instead, we rely on browser localStorage (as described above) which provides similar functionality without the tracking concerns associated with traditional cookies. LocalStorage data:

  • Never gets sent to our servers or any third parties
  • Persists only until you clear your browser data
  • Is inaccessible to other websites due to same-origin policy
  • Cannot be used for cross-site tracking

You won't see any cookie consent banners on aqimap.fun because there's nothing to consent to‚Äîwe simply don't use cookies.
