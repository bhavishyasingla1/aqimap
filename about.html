About AQIMap.fun

Making air quality data accessible, visual, and actionable for everyone around the world.

üåç What is AQIMap.fun?

AQIMap.fun is a cutting-edge, interactive web platform designed to democratize access to real-time air quality information across the globe. Unlike traditional environmental monitoring tools that present data in complex scientific formats, we transform air quality index (AQI) readings into intuitive, visual representations that anyone can understand at a glance.

Our platform covers over 195 countries worldwide, aggregating data from thousands of environmental monitoring stations to provide you with the most accurate and up-to-date air quality information available. Whether you're planning your daily commute, considering a vacation destination, or researching global pollution trends, AQIMap.fun delivers the insights you need in a beautiful, easy-to-navigate interface.

The air quality index is more than just a number‚Äîit's a critical health indicator that affects billions of people every day. At AQI Map.fun, we believe that everyone deserves to know what they're breathing, and we're committed to making that information as accessible as checking the weather forecast.

üéØ Our Mission

Air pollution is one of the most pressing environmental health challenges of our time, responsible for millions of premature deaths annually according to the World Health Organization. Yet, many people remain unaware of the air quality in their immediate environment or how it compares to other regions worldwide.

Our mission at AQIMap.fun is threefold:

  • Raise Awareness: By visualizing air quality data on an interactive world map with real-time color-coded indicators, we make pollution levels immediately visible and understandable to everyone, regardless of their scientific background.
  • Empower Action: Knowledge is power. When people understand the air quality in their area, they can make informed decisions about outdoor activities, exercise routines, travel plans, and even long-term decisions about where to live and work.
  • Drive Change: By making air pollution data transparent and accessible, we hope to inspire individuals, communities, and policymakers to take meaningful action toward cleaner air and a healthier planet.

We envision a world where checking air quality is as routine as checking the weather, where clean air is recognized as a fundamental human right, and where data-driven awareness leads to collective action for environmental improvement.

üí° What Makes AQIMap.fun Different?

In a crowded field of air quality monitoring tools, AQIMap.fun stands out through several innovative features and design philosophies:

Cigarette Equivalents - Making AQI Relatable

Traditional AQI numbers (0-500) can feel abstract. What does an AQI of 150 really mean for your health? We solve this by converting AQI values into cigarette equivalents based on Berkeley Earth's research. When you see that breathing the air in a city is equivalent to smoking 2.5 cigarettes per day, the health impact becomes immediately clear and personal.

Real-Time Global Coverage

Unlike regional or city-specific air quality apps, AQIMap.fun provides a bird's-eye view of air pollution across the entire planet. Our integration with the World Air Quality Index (WAQI) project gives us access to data from over 12,000 monitoring stations in 1,000+ cities across 195+ countries, updated in real-time.

Premium, Intuitive Design

We believe that important environmental data deserves a beautiful presentation. Our glassmorphism-inspired interface, smooth animations, and color-coded visual system make exploring air quality data not just informative but genuinely enjoyable. The platform features both light and dark modes, ensuring comfortable viewing at any time of day.

Zero Barriers to Entry

No sign-up required. No app installation needed. No complicated dashboards to navigate. Just visit aqimap.fun, click on any country, and instantly see its air quality status. We've optimized the platform to work seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, making air quality data accessible wherever you are.

SEO-Optimized Country Pages

Each country on AQIMap.fun has its own dedicated URL (like aqimap.fun/india or aqimap.fun/united-states), making it easy to share specific air quality information and helping people discover regional data through search engines.

üìä Data Sources & Accuracy

All air quality data on AQIMap.fun is sourced from the World Air Quality Index (WAQI) project, a comprehensive global air pollution database established in 2007. WAQI aggregates real-time air quality data from environmental protection agencies and monitoring stations worldwide, including:

  • EPA (United States Environmental Protection Agency)
  • CPCB (Central Pollution Control Board, India)
  • MEE (Ministry of Ecology and Environment, China)
  • EEA (European Environment Agency)
  • Hundreds of other governmental and NGO sources

The data is updated every hourly for most stations, with some high-traffic locations updating as frequently as every 15 minutes. We display the most recent available data and cache it for 30 minutes to balance freshness with API efficiency.

For countries or regions where real-time data is unavailable, we provide 2-year historical averages to ensure comprehensive global coverage. These fallback values are clearly marked in the interface.
